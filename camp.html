Calculus Camp

An online, two-week crash course covering the basics of single-variable limits, derivatives, and integrals with a nontraditional approach. This camp is not a standard high school calculus course. Rather, it will teach the relevant concepts in an intuitive manner before transitioning to more rigorous proof-based presentations. The goal is for all students to gain an understanding and appreciation of the elegance of calculus.

Prerequisites: A solid background in algebra and trigonometry. Completion of a standard precalculus course is recommended but not required. The application includes a short diagnostic that all applicants must fill out.
