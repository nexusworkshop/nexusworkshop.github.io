Let the operation $\bigstar$ be defined by $x\bigstar y=y^x-xy$. Calculate $(3\bigstar4)-(4\bigstar3)$.

Solution
We have $$\begin{align*} (3\bigstar4)-(4\bigstar3) &=(4^3 - 3\cdot 4) - (3^4 - 4\cdot 3) \\ &= (64 - 12) - (81 - 12) \\ &= \boxed{-17} \end{align*}$$
