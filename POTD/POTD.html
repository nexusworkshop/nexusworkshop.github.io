Problem of The Day

Welcome to POTD!

Guidelines

  • Each day before noon EST, a new problem from a past math competition will be posted in our Discord server.
  • Problem difficulty increases each day of the week (Monday is easiest, Sunday is hardest).
  • Pleaes DM @The Nexus Workshop to answer. You will have 3 attempts per problem. Since "The Nexus Workshop" is not a bot, someone will manually check your answer. You should expect a response within 3 hours unless it is the middle of the night EST.
  • You can submit until the next POTD is posted.
  • No consulting outside resources or asking for help.
  • Please do not discuss the current POTD other than in #potd-solvers
  • Coding and calculators are strictly NOT allowed unless otherwise specified
  • Please do NOT edit your submission.
