Join Our Team

Here are the positions we're currently offering! Please apply here if you're interested in becoming a member of the Nexus team. Descriptions of each position along with prerequisites and estimated time commitment are provided below.

Front End Developer

Description: Collaborate with others to develop new features and update our website.

Prerequisites: Web development experience with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript are required.

Estimated Time Commitment: ~ 3 hours per week (very flexible).

Content Creator

Description: Collaborate with others to write articles for our website.

Prerequisites: AIME qualification or the international equivalent is required. Also, experience with LaTeX is required. Experience with HTML is recommended, but not necessary.

Estimated Time Commitment: ~ 3 hours per week (very flexible).

Calculus Camp Instructor

Description: Help run discussions for our 2021 calculus camp in our Discord server.

Prerequisites: 5 on AP Calculus BC exam is required, although exceptions can be made on an individual basis. Experience with LaTeX is required.

Estimated Time Commitment: ~ 5-10 hours per week leading up to and during the camp (which will run from June 28-July 9).

Discord Moderator

Description: Moderate our Discord server and take care of POTD. This could potentially involve:

  • Posting problems in the server in the morning
  • Writing short solutions
  • Keeping track of scoring

Prerequisites: Solid people skills, AIME qualification or the international equivalent is recommended but not necessary.

Estimated Time Commitment: ~ 1-3 hours per week (very very flexible).
